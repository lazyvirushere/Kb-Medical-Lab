Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How do I book a test?

You can book a test online through our website, call us directly, or visit any of our branches. We also offer home sample collection services for your convenience.

How long does it take to get results?

Most routine tests are completed within 24 hours. Specialized tests may take 2-3 days. You'll receive results via email, SMS, or can collect them from our lab.

Do you offer home sample collection?

Yes! We provide home sample collection services in major cities. Our trained phlebotomists will visit your home at your preferred time to collect samples.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and mobile payments. Online booking also supports various payment gateways for your convenience.

Are your tests accurate and reliable?

Absolutely! We use state-of-the-art equipment and follow international standards. Our lab is ISO 15189 certified and our results are trusted by leading hospitals across Pakistan.
